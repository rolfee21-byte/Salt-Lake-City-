🍽️ Food & Drink in SLC

From James Beard-nominated chefs to legendary hole-in-the-wall taquerias — SLC's culinary scene punches way above its weight.

Craft Beer

SLC's Craft Brewery Scene

Despite Utah's unique alcohol laws, SLC has developed one of the most creative craft beer cultures in the Mountain West.

⚠️ Utah Alcohol Quick Guide

🍺 Grocery stores sell beer up to 5% ABV
🍷 Wine & spirits from DABC State Liquor Stores only
🍹 Bars & restaurants serve full-strength alcohol
🏪 DABC stores are closed on Sundays
🍻 Craft breweries can sell high-point beer on-site
⏰ Last call is 1am at most venues
